Function Details

area/3

area(Shape::atom(), A::number(), B::number()) -> number()

Calculates the area of a geometric shape, given the shape and two of its dimensions. For a rectangle it returns the products of its arguments, for a triangle one half the product of its arguments, and for an ellipse math:pi() times the product of the arguments.
